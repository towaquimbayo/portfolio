MyMind

Overview

A mental health-focused web application, where patients can purchase therapy sessions offered by therapists. Administrators have access to the dashboard that displays all users in the database where they can perform all CRUD (Create Read Update Delete) operations on the database. Scalability to update their session details and perform a live chat session with their patients when they purchase a session in an e-commerce-based transaction process. Our project won total of 2 awards for the "Best Design" and "Best Teamwork" honourable mention for the "Most Innovative" out of 200+ students (Source Article).
