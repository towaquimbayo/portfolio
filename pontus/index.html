Pontus

Overview

Pontus is a Vancouver, Canada based agritech and vertical aquaponics operation that licenses the expertise to build and operate aqsaponics farms that specialize in the organic and sustainable production of water lentils and water lentil protein powder. Their mission is to provide the world with an environmentally sustainable source of pure plant-based protein and serves to cater to the growing consumer base shifting from meat-based diets to plant-based diets.
